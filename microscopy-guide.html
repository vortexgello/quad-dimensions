    3. Advanced Microscopy Techniques

    Phase Contrast

    Enhances contrast in transparent specimens without staining. Best for live cells.

    DIC (Nomarski)

    Provides pseudo-3D relief images. Useful for detailed organelle visualization.

    Darkfield

    Highlights edges and particles in thin, transparent specimens via scattered light.

    Multiphoton

    Infrared-based deep tissue fluorescence with reduced phototoxicity.

    SPM (AFM / STM)

    Surface scanning for nanotechnology and materials research.

    Portable / Handheld

    Forensics, field inspection, or rapid low-mag documentation.
